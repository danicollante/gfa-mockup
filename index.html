About GFA

Strategic advisory in frontier markets.

Creating strategic advantage on a global scale.

Global Frontier Advisors is a U.S.-based strategic advisory firm helping corporations and investors navigate complexity and unlock opportunity in the world's most challenging environments.

Operating at the intersection of national security, economic resilience, and frontier market development, we advise organizations in sectors critical to U.S. and allied interests—including critical minerals, energy, infrastructure, defense, and artificial intelligence.

The firm is led by senior leaders across national security, foreign policy, and private industry, including two former three-star U.S. Marine Corps generals, the former U.S. Ambassador to Venezuela, and entrepreneurs who have built and operated companies in frontier markets for decades.

GFA was built for this moment

Geopolitical competition, the race for artificial intelligence, the reshoring of allied supply chains, and the drive to reduce strategic dependence on foreign adversaries are reshaping markets and redefining U.S. commercial diplomacy. GFA helps clients identify opportunities created by this global realignment, align their strategies with long-term policy and market trends, and execute with confidence in complex operating environments.

Our mission

Advancing national interest through private enterprise.

The firm was built by leaders who spent their careers in service to the United States — and who never stopped believing in the strategic value of American engagement abroad.

We believe responsibly-led private enterprise is one of the most effective instruments of national strategy.

Our principals served in uniform, in embassies, and across the interagency. That perspective shapes how we work today. We help American and allied enterprises compete and operate in strategically significant regions — aligning commercial objectives with the broader interest in stable, secure, and open markets.

Where capital, security, and diplomacy intersect, we help our clients move with confidence and credibility. Success in a frontier market is rarely only a commercial outcome; done well, it strengthens the relationships and supply chains that matter far beyond a single transaction.
